# Purpose

This document captures the long-term vision for APForce beyond the
current roadmap.

It is intentionally aspirational. Items listed here are ideas, strategic
directions, and future opportunities---not committed features.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Product Vision

APForce will evolve from a WhatsApp CRM into a complete AI-powered
Business Operating System.

The platform should become the central workspace where businesses
acquire customers, communicate, automate operations, analyze
performance, and use AI to increase productivity.

# AI Platform

## AI Inbox

-   Reply suggestions
-   Conversation summaries
-   Intent detection
-   Smart knowledge retrieval
-   Suggested next actions

## AI Chat with Customers

Full multi-turn AI conversation with an end customer over WhatsApp.
Blocked on a Knowledge Center (does not exist yet — see the 2026-07-05
AI audit). Pre-implementation design requirements — autonomous-by-
default with approval as a safety net, a superadmin-only per-
conversation exchange cap, and an intent-first routing shortcut before
ever starting a full AI conversation — are recorded in
`docs/adr/ADR-016-ai-chat-design-requirements.md`. Read that ADR before
starting this feature.

## AI Campaigns

-   Audience recommendations
-   AI-written campaign content
-   Best send-time prediction
-   Performance optimization

## AI Automation

-   Workflow generation
-   AI rule suggestions
-   AI troubleshooting
-   Workflow optimization

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Innovation Principles

Every new idea should satisfy at least one goal:

-   Save customer time
-   Increase automation
-   Improve reliability
-   Improve customer experience
-   Increase scalability
-   Reduce operational cost

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Success Definition

APForce succeeds when customers spend less time managing software and
more time growing their business.

The platform should become an intelligent business partner---not just
another CRM.
